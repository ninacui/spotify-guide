Sharing spotify playlists

Playlists are automatically public when theyâ€™re created. You can also make playlists secret. You would be the only person able to see the playlist.

To share a playlist:

  1. Right-click on a particular playlist and a pop-up window with the Share option appears. Click share to see options to share a playlist via Facebook, Messenger, Twitter, Telegram, Skype or Tumblr.
  2. You can also share the playlist by clicking Copy Playlist Link or Copy Spotify URL to share via other means.

Collaborative playlist

Spotify's collaboration feature lets your friends add and remove tracks in your playlist.

To turn collaborative playlist option:

  1. Right-click the playlist you want to share or collaborate with others.
  2. Click Collaborative Playlist.

    3. Note: You can only turn on collaboration on playlists made by you.
