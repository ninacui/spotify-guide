Starting a playlist

To create an empty playlist in Spotify

  1. Click New Playlist.
  2. Fill in Name and Description fields.
  3. Click Create.
    4. The side navigation menu can be hard to find.
  4. A “Create Playlist” window will appear. Enter the name and definition of your playlist. Definition is optional.

Note: You can name your playlist whatever you’d like, but it may be best to give it a name relevant to the songs you include for better organization. This will also make your playlist easier to find for other listeners.
Your new playlist will now display.
