What is a Spotify playlist?

A playlist is a list of songs you can make, share, and enjoy listening. Spotify allows you to listen to your own playlists as well as others’ playlists.

Types of playlists

Personalized playlists (called “Made for You” playlists) are auto-generated by Spotify and based on your listening habits. Spotify remembers what you like, share, save, and skip.

  • These playlists include your Daily Mix, Discover Weekly, Release Radar, and On Repeat and Repeat Rewind. You can find them in YOUR LIBRARY.
  • Editorial playlists are made by Spotify’s Editorial team, and are based on genre on mood. Find them in Browse.
  • Listener playlists are those that are created by Spotify users. They are extremely customizable.

What can you do with playlists?

Aside from listening to playlists, users can also collaborate on playlists and share them with others.
Learn more about making a collaborative playlist and sharing playlists through the guide.
