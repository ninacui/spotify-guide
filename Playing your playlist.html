Playing a playlist

Play options

Spotify enables the user to play the tracks basis two options.

Shuffle

Shuffle play is a mode of music playback when you do not want to follow the order of songs set up in a playlist. It prevents repeated tracks. You can turn shuffle on for any playlist, album, or artist profile

  1. Click the ‘Shuffle’ icon while playing a song from any playlist. Clicking it to enable the shuffle option. Clicking the icon again to switch off the shuffling.

Repeat songs

This option helps you to repeat the current track or the whole playlist without clicking on the song again and again.

To repeat the current track:

  1. Click the repeat icon once. To turn it off, click the repeat icon again.

Queue

You can edit or add songs to your queue on Spotify and control which songs play next.

To view the queue

Click the queue icon in the ‘Now Playing’ bar.
The Play Queue page displays. You can view which songs are playing next, and edit the upcoming list.

To add to the queue

Click, hold, and drag a song to reorder.

To remove from the queue

Right-click on a song and select Remove from Queue.
