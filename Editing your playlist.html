Editing an existing playlist

You may realize at one point that you would like to make changes to your playlist, organize it, or delete it entirely.

Removing songs from your playlist

To remove a song from your playlist:

  1. Open your playlist.
  2. Hover your mouse over the song you would like to remove. A … icon displays on the far right of the song.
  3. Click the … icon. A menu displays.
  4. Click Remove from this playlist. Your playlist no longer contains this song.

You may realize at one point that you would like to make changes to your playlist, organize it, or delete it entirely.

Editing a playlist’s details

To rename a playlist or change its description:

  1. Open your playlist.
  2. Click on the … button on the right of the Play button. A menu appears.
  3. Click on Edit Details. A window displays with text boxes for your title and description.
  4. Make your desired changes and click Save.

Deleting a playlist

To delete a playlist:

  1. Open your playlist.
  2. Click on the … button on the right of the Play button. A menu appears.
  3. Click on Delete. Your playlist is now deleted.

Alternately, you can also use the following method:

  1. Right-click your playlist in the left menu bar of the window.
  2. Click Delete.

Organizing playlists

After you login to Spotify desktop version, the left navigation menu shows an option with the header ‘playlist’ with a list of all your saved and created playlists. Clicking on a particular playlist will take you to the list of songs added up to that playlist.

  • Click on the playlist name to go to the playlist.
  • Click, hold, and drag a playlist to move it up or down.

Playlist folders

To create a new playlist folder and rename it:

  1. Hover the mouse on a particular playlist.
  2. Select the ‘Create folder’ option to add the particular playlist to the folder.
  3. By-default, Spotify will name it as ‘New Folder.’ Double-click ‘New Folder’ to rename it.
  4. Click and drag a playlist to add it to the folder.
