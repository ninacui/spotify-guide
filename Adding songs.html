Adding and downloading songs to your playlist

Adding songs

After you create an empty playlist, Spotify will display a list of recommended songs based on the title and description of your playlist.

To add recommended songs to your playlist:

  1. Click the ... icon on a song.
  2. Click ‘Add Playlist’ to add the song to a new playlist or the already created playlist.

To add other songs to your playlist:

  1. Use the search bar on the top left of the window to find a song, album, or artist. You can also explore with the Home, Browse, and Radio sections on the left side of the window.
  2. Once you have selected the song, use the … button to add the song to a playlist.

Liked songs

By default Spotify creates a playlist of your liked songs with the name Liked Songs. If you want to keep your songs in one place, you can access the Liked songs tab. This can be seen under Your library in the left-side menu.

To add songs to Liked songs playlist:

The left side of the songs shows a heart icon. Click on the heart to add the song to the Liked songs playlist.

Downloading songs

Note: You can only download songs if you have purchased Spotify Premium.
You can only download songs in a playlist.

To download all songs in a playlist:
Click the Download switch button.

After you download the songs in a playlist, you can listen to them when you’re offline.
